About Me

I’ve always had a knack for turning chaos into clarity—especially when it comes to testing processes. With over 20 years of experience in QA automation across industries like finance, healthcare, and cybersecurity, I specialize in designing scalable, maintainable test automation frameworks.

At Searchlight Cyber, I architected a complete QA function from scratch, integrating test automation and Agile processes that significantly improved defect detection and streamlined software releases. My time at Xe.com saw me leading the development of a Cypress TypeScript boilerplate, which not only enhanced test coverage but also created a more efficient, collaborative testing culture.

I’m a firm believer that great automation isn’t just about writing tests—it’s about enabling teams to ship with confidence. That’s why I focus on maintainability, scalability, and CI/CD integration, ensuring automated tests contribute to a frictionless development lifecycle. Whether it’s adopting Playwright for robust UI testing, implementing API testing strategies, or coaching teams on automation best practices, I love finding ways to make testing smarter, not harder.

Beyond my hands-on work, I stay engaged in the tech community, sharing insights on automation trends, and continuously learning. If you're passionate about improving software quality and efficiency, let’s connect and exchange ideas.
